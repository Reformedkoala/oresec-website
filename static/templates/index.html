Colorado School of Mines Cybersecurity Club

How to Join?

To join OreSec, simply show up to our meetings and involve yourself in the club. If you're new to cyber security, just show up eager to learn!

You should join our Discord in order to find out what's going on and see our upcoming events. One you join head over to the #announcements channel.

What do we do?

The Colorado School of Mines Cybersecurity Club (OreSec) competes in Capture-the-Flag competitions both in-person and online, as well as Attack-Defense competitions, and various others. We also have typically weekly meetings and talks about Cybersecurity related topics. If you're completely new this is a great place to get involved and learn more!
