How to Join?

To join OreSec you can join our OreConnect Group and our Discord then just show up to any meeting!

We are open to any skill and interest level!

Our main form of communication will be through Discord and we've got a healthy community that you can chat and ask questions in!

We also will be using OreConnect to communicate the same information, gauge interest, and give out badges!

What do we do?

[Industry and Academic Talks]

OreSec regularly hosts talks from both Industry professionals and Academics alike.


These are advertised in our Discord and often include free food and allow you to interact and ask questions!

[Competitions]

OreSec does not have an official CTF team, which we believe is a good thing!

This allows anyone and everyone to join any CTF we are participating in as long as there is enough space and no restrictions on number of teams!

So keep up with our Discord to see upcoming competition oppurtunities.

[HSOreCTF]

OreSec has started a new tradition of hosting an annual High School CTF called HSOreCTF.

Wanna get involved? Keep up with our Discord!

[Hacking Nights and Cyber Range]

Coming this year OreSec is working on implementing a Cyber Range on the Mines campus. This will eventually allow OreSec members to practice their attack or defense skills!

We need architects for machines! If helping out with the Cyber Range sounds fun to you, make sure you join our Discord!

In conjunction with this and our normal meetings, OreSec will be hosting weekly hacking nights on Mondays at 8:00 PM MDT in CTLM B60! These will range from HackTheBox walkthroughs or work-alongs to full-blown Hardware Hacking nights.

If you're interested keep up with the Discord and show up!
